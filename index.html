Time of Day

A JavaScript Project by Sarah Keane

Greeting goes here

This page checks what the hour is, and then sets the background color, greeting, and picture based on it.

It checks whether it is morning (5am to 12 noon), afternoon (12 noon to 6pm), evening (6pm to 11pm), or night (11pm to 5am).

Also, the copyright date in the footer updates as the years go by. and, if the current year is the same as the original copyright year, it will only show the year once! (instead of showing it as "2023-2023")
